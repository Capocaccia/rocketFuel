Who We Are

Cottage on Main, established in 2012 by owners Vicky Stewart and Kelly Pollard, is a home furnishings store specializing in French country and classic Southern design.

Tucked away in a recently restored, 100 year old cottage in charming historic downtown Collierville, Tennessee, our store provides an original shopping experience. The home itself, with its beautiful setting, original pine floors, three fireplaces, and abundant natural light allows you to determine how design elements will work in your own home

Expect to discover our selection of fine furniture, home decor and accessories, designer lighting, artwork and so much more when you visit Cottage on Main
